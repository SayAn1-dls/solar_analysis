# Renewable Utilization Playbook

The goal of solar optimization is not only to avoid deficits but also to maximize useful consumption of available renewable power. Strong utilization reduces wasted generation and lowers fossil backup dependency.

Best practices:
- Prioritize direct consumption of midday solar before scheduling imports.
- Route surplus energy into storage, thermal loads, or planned flexible processes.
- Build utilization plans around the forecast peak hour and the hours immediately around it.
- Use the forecast summary to communicate when operators should expect surplus, normal coverage, or constrained periods.

Operational note:
An optimization report should include both balancing actions for risk windows and utilization actions for high-generation windows.
