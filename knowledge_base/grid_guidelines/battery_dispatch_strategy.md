# Battery Dispatch Strategy

Battery storage should absorb midday solar surplus and discharge during late afternoon or evening deficits. Charging decisions must respect power limits, state-of-charge limits, and efficiency losses.

Best practices:
- Charge storage during surplus windows instead of curtailing usable solar output.
- Reserve a portion of stored energy for the first high-risk deficit period rather than fully cycling too early.
- Match charge-discharge plans to the maximum inverter or battery power rating.
- Use storage to smooth ramp-down events when forecasted solar output falls quickly.

Operational note:
Battery actions are most effective when paired with demand scheduling so the battery is not solving every imbalance alone.
