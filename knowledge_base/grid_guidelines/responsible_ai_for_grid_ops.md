# Responsible AI For Grid Ops

AI-generated recommendations should support grid operators, not replace operational authority. Forecasting and optimization outputs contain uncertainty and must be validated against live measurements and local operating procedures.

Best practices:
- Keep a human in the loop for dispatch, curtailment, and reserve decisions.
- Show supporting references and risk assumptions with every recommendation.
- Avoid overclaiming precision when the model is extrapolating beyond observed conditions.
- Log scenario inputs, forecast assumptions, and recommended actions for auditability.

Operational note:
A trustworthy assistant explains why it is recommending an action and clearly marks when data limitations could reduce confidence.
